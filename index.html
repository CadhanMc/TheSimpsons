The Simpsons Arcade

Little tribute to The Simpsons arcade game developed with C++ and SDL library.

The tribute consists of the first stage of The Simpsons Arcade game. It consists of leading Homer through the streets of Downtown Springfield that is full of animations and characters of the TV Series, such as, Skinner or Martin. By playing this game you will be able to hit and jump with Homer.
